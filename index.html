Instructions

1. Select a distribution type: ERC-20 token or native coin (ETH/BNB/MATIC/FTM/etc).

2. Select your target network (Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Fantom, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, or Avalanche).

3. Click "Switch Wallet Network" to match your wallet to the selected chain.

4. For ERC-20 mode: enter multisender contract + token contract, then click "Approve Tokens".

5. For ERC-20 mode: provide lines in address,weight format and enter total tokens to distribute.

6. For native mode: provide lines in address,amount format and enter total native coins to distribute.

7. Click "Distribute" and confirm transactions in your wallet.

8. Verify resulting transfers in your selected network explorer.
