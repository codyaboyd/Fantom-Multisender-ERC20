Instructions

1. Find the contract address for the token you wish to dstribute.

2. Copy and paste it into the "Token Contract" field in the correct format.

3. Click the approve button and fire the transaction and wait for it to confirm.

4. Enter each wallet you would like to send tokens to, followed by a comma and then the amount of FTM that wallet previously sent to you for their OTC contribution.

5. Use a new line for each wallet address and its respective amount of FTM.

6. Input the total number of tokens that you are distributing as a collective total in the "Tokens To Distribute" field.

7. The FractalAir dapp will calculate each wallets share of tokens according to the formula: FTM From Wallet/Total FTM Sum.

7. Click the DISTRIBUTE button and fire off the transaction.

8. All tokens should be distributed accordingly once the transaction confirms.

9. Check on FTMscan to confirm the distribution was successful.
